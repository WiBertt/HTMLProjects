Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F or 180C
  2. Mix the milk, yeast, sugar and eggs together in a bowl
  3. Add the butter and mix until combined
  4. Add the flour and mix until combined
  5. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
  6. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap
  7. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours
  8. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a large rectangle
  9. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon
